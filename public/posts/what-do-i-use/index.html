What do I use?.

I use a lot of Tools, Apps, and Services to get by every day. I’ve listed down those that I love and use frequently here.

Services.

  • Zoho Mail - as my email host
  • Duck Duck Go - to Search.
  • Google Domains - to get and manage domains.
  • Airtable - as a backend for my experiments and side projects.
  • Netlify and Firebase - to Host.

Apps.

  • Youtube Music - to listen to music.
  • Firefox - as my main browser.
  • Pocket - to save and read later.
  • Authy - for 2FA as a Google Authenticator replacement.
  • Spark - as my email client.
  • Zoho Vault - to manage Passwords.

Gear.

  • iPhone 11 - as my daily driver.
  • OnePlus 6 - for testing my apps.
  • MacBook Pro M1 Max 14" - as my primary computer.
  • AirPods Pro - to hear music.
  • Apple Watch Series 6 - as a fitness tracker.
